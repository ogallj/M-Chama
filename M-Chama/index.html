Powerful Features for Community Savings

M-Chama combines traditional community savings practices with modern digital technology to create a seamless experience.

Create & Join Savings Groups

Easily create new savings groups or join existing ones. Set group rules, contribution schedules, and membership requirements.

Schedule Contributions

Set up regular contribution schedules - weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Track contribution history and upcoming payments.

SMS Payment Reminders

Never miss a payment with automated SMS reminders. Get notified about upcoming contributions and group activities.

M-PESA Integration

Seamlessly connect with M-PESA for easy deposits and withdrawals. Secure transactions with real-time confirmation.

Group Loan Management

Manage group loans with customizable terms. Track loan applications, approvals, repayments, and interest calculations.

Instant Withdrawals

Access your funds when you need them with instant withdrawals to your M-PESA account. No delays, no paperwork.

How M-Chama Works

Get started with M-Chama in just a few simple steps and transform how your community saves together.

1

Download & Register

Download the M-Chama app from Google Play Store or App Store. Register using your phone number and verify with M-PESA.

2

Create or Join a Group

Start your own savings group or join an existing one using an invitation code. Set group rules and contribution amounts.

3

Make Contributions

Contribute to your group savings using M-PESA. Set up automatic payments or make manual contributions as needed.

4

Manage Group Finances

Track group savings, apply for loans, and withdraw funds directly to your M-PESA account when needed.
